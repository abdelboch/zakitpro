## LinkedIn Caption

Managing Intune Windows Update Rings manually is slow.
Blindly letting AI change them is worse.

I published a practical guide for using AI to automate Intune update ring work **without** wiping out the settings that actually protect production:

- define Pilot, Broad, and VIP intent first
- protect blackout dates and exclusions before prompting
- force AI to use explicit Graph fields instead of guessed defaults
- review the JSON payload before PATCH/POST
- validate the read-back in Intune after every change

AI is great at accelerating script creation.
It is terrible at understanding your business calendar, ring strategy, and political blast radius.

That means desktop engineers still own the guardrails.

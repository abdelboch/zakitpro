## My recommendation for most Zakitpro readers

For most desktop engineers, the smartest sequence is:

1. complete one free official Microsoft AI credential or foundational path
2. decide whether AI is actually part of your career direction
3. then choose the paid path that matches your ecosystem

In practice, that usually means:

- Microsoft-heavy reader: free Microsoft Applied Skills → AI-900
- cloud-curious reader: free AWS path → AWS Certified AI Practitioner
- advanced implementation reader: AI-900 first, then AI-102 if role direction justifies it

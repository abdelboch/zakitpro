AI is terrible at writing Intune detection rules. There, I said it. 🛑

I’ve seen ChatGPT confidently recommend checking `HKCU` for a System-context app deployment. Spoiler alert: That’s going to fail across your entire fleet, resulting in constant retry loops.

Why? Because the AI doesn’t naturally differentiate between user context and system context unless you explicitly drill it into the prompt.

Here’s the golden rule for AI-generated detection scripts: NEVER trust its registry hive paths or environment variables without double-checking the execution context.

If it’s a system install, stick to `HKLM` and `C:\Program Files`. 

Use AI to build the framework, but you must supply the engineering brains.

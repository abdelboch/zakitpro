Autopilot ESP failures are one of the fastest ways to lose an entire morning.

Not because the fix is always hard.
Because the evidence is usually scattered across the ESP phase, Intune app status, detection logic, and whatever changed last.

I just published a practical workflow for using AI to triage Windows Autopilot ESP failures faster without trusting hallucinated fixes.

What matters:
- identify the exact ESP phase first
- build a clean evidence packet before prompting
- ask AI for ranked hypotheses, not magic answers
- validate packaging, targeting, detection, and network assumptions in Intune
- use AI to tighten escalation notes when the issue needs a handoff

This is where AI is useful for desktop engineers: compressing noisy evidence into a better next-step plan.
